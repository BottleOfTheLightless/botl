Bottle of the Lightless

Collect a diverse roster of heroes, each with their own unique abilities and strengths. Travel across the Blighted Dark, in search of the Bottle of the Lightless, a relic of immense power that has been lost to the world for centuries. The Bottle is said to be the source of the Blight, a dark force that has consumed the land and twisted its inhabitants into monstrous creatures. Only by finding the Bottle and destroying it can the Blight be stopped and the world saved from destruction.

Only the best heroes will return, Bottle in hand. Others will perish in the Blighted Dark, consumed by the darkness that lurks within.
